Quizzes

These quizzes are designed to assess your knowledge on the key concepts required to flourish in this society. Quiz 1 will assess your knowledge on Electro-Magnetism and the fundamentals of spacecraft propulsion. Quiz 2 will plasma physics and pulsed plasma thrusters (PPTs). Quiz 3 covers the knowledge needed to understand Chathodes and Hall Effect Thrusters (HETs). Finally Quiz 4 will cover the Magnetic Nozzle Thrusters and the basic principles of diagnostics. All quizzess can be attempted as many times as you wish and there is no time limit. Good Luck!

Mini Project Assingment

An exciting assignment has been made, allowing members to test their abilities, creativity and knowledge. One of five random briefs will be assigned to you and your task is to design and document a solution to the given problem. Your design and documentation skills will be assessed and feedback provided based on the mark scheme below. We are assessing members abilities to analyse a problem, find a solution and document to a high engineering standard. This task is still meant to be fun and builds skills that are expected not only in this society, but in University and work life!
