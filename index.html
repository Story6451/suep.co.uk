Welcome!

On this website you will find many documents and research about the many engineering projects that is being carried out by the Southampton University Electric Propulsion team or SUEP for short. We also hope to teach anyone and everyone who is interested in electric propulsion.

SUEP is a team within the Southampton University Spaceflight socieity (SUSF), and we have the opportunity to talk with high level academics, and use facilities available to us at the University of Southampton. We give acess to our research and resources for free to make electric propulsion more accessible! If spacecraft engineering and experimental development of new thruster technologies sounds interesting, consider joining or at least checking us out.
