About the SUEP initiative

SUEP (Southampton University Electric Propulsion), associated with SUSF, was the first student team to test-fire a Hall-effect thruster in the UK. Our ambitions are to make EP more accessible to students and pursue innovative projects that will teach and test new technologies.
